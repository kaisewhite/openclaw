## Flight Guidance

### Recommended Airport
- **Fly into Geneva (GVA), Switzerland** — this is the closest major airport to Méribel.

### Arrival (Jan 30)
- **Land between 7:00–9:00 AM local time** at GVA to make the shuttle.
- Shuttles are scheduled to depart Geneva Airport around **~10:00 AM** local time.
- Drive time to Méribel: ~3–4 hours.

### Optional Early Arrival (Jan 29)
- Most of the group arrives a day early (Jan 29) into Geneva and spends the night.
- This gives a buffer against delays and a chance to hang out before heading up.

### Departure (Feb 7)
- Shuttle departs the resort early morning (~06:00 AM based on last year).
- Book flights departing GVA **11:00 AM or later** to have plenty of time.

### NYC Reference (Last Year)
- **Outbound:** UA 956 — EWR → GVA (evening departure, morning arrival)
- **Return:** UA 9720 — GVA → JFK (11:40 AM departure)
- These or similar-timed flights work well for the NYC crew.
